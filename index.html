About Hotel Jofa

Welcome to Migori's Premier Luxury Destination

Nestled in the serene landscapes of Mabera, Migori County, Hotel Jofa stands as a testament to elegance, comfort, and world-class hospitality. Our 8-star establishment offers an unparalleled experience that blends modern luxury with authentic Kenyan charm.

Under the expert management of Ian Chacha, our dedicated team ensures every guest receives personalized attention and experiences the highest standards of service. Whether you're visiting for business or leisure, Hotel Jofa promises memories that will last a lifetime.
